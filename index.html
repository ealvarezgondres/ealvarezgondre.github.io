About Me

Graphic Designer & Developer

Hello!

My name is Esther Alvarez Gondres, I am dedicated to my work where I have always been fascinated by and see how the world is represented through the eyes of others. My main interest is to carry out experiences through designing around various aspects of visual and spatial perception from everyday objects and situations while exploring its concepts. Becoming a designer has opened up the possibilities of expressing myself into a very wide variety of media with which I feel comfortable and not afraid to take a risk of using. Also, practicing in different areas of knowledge has allowed me to integrate a multidisciplinary vision towards the problems that I address in art my work.
